Training Program

Our training program offers customized lessons and training for riders and horses of all experience levels. We also provide horses for sale and lease options, ensuring that every rider finds the perfect match for their needs.

Riding Programs:
    Comprehensive Programs: Designed to meet the needs of both pleasure and competitive riders.
    Safety & Rider Development: Emphasis on safety and developing strong riding skills.
    Daily Group & Private Classes: Held by appointment, with lessons available on your own horse or one of our reliable lesson horses and ponies.
Program Highlights:
    Students are divided into groups by age and ability.
    Activities are tailored to the level of the student.
    Each student is assigned a different horse daily for riding, handling, and feeding.
    Hands-on horse activities such as grooming, tacking, bathing, clipping, and wrapping are done daily.
    Special equipment and aids, such as a full-sized horse replica, are used for demonstrations to make learning safe and fun.

Boarding

At Mad Maple Farm we are committed to providing top-notch boarding services that cater to the needs of both horses and their owners. Our comprehensive boarding package includes:

    >Hay and Grain Feeding: Ensuring your horse receives a balanced and nutritious diet.
    Turnout: Regular turnout to promote physical health and mental well-being.
    Stall Mucking: Daily cleaning to maintain a clean and comfortable environment.
    Boots and Blanketing: Personalized care to keep your horse protected and comfortable in all weather conditions.
Facilities:
    Spacious Stalls: Large stalls equipped with water outlets for easy access to fresh water.
    Wash and Grooming Stalls: Five large hot and cold water wash stalls and grooming stalls available for boarders.
    Tack Rooms: Ample storage with large tack rooms, additional blanket, and trunk storage.
    Trail Access: Enjoy access to acres of scenic trails for riding and exploration.
Benefits of Boarding at Mad Maple Farm:
    Health and Well-being: Regular turnout and personalized care contribute to your horse's physical and mental health.
    Convenience: On-site amenities such as wash stalls and tack rooms make horse care more convenient for owners.
    Community: Join a community of passionate equestrians and enjoy the camaraderie and support of fellow horse enthusiasts.
    Safety: Our facility is designed with safety in mind, providing a secure and comfortable environment for your horse.

Horse Leasing:

Leasing a horse provides a unique opportunity to experience the benefits of horse ownership without the full commitment. Our leasing options are designed to cater to riders of all levels, offering flexibility and affordability.

Types of Leases:
    Full Lease: Exclusive use of the horse, with the lessee responsible for all care and expenses.
    Partial Lease: Shared use and expenses of the horse with the owner or other lessees.
    On-Site Lease: The horse remains at our facility, and you ride there.
    Off-Site Lease: You can move the horse to a facility of your choice.
Benefits of Leasing:
    Lower Financial Commitment: Leasing is more affordable than buying a horse outright.
    Flexibility: Allows you to ride and care for a horse without a long-term commitment.
    Experience: Gain valuable experience in horse care and riding before deciding on full ownership.
    Variety: Opportunity to ride different horses and disciplines as your skills improve.


Consultancy Services:
Leveraged by our decades of expertise in managing, purchasing, leasing, and operating businesses related to English-style horseback riding. Our comprehensive consultancy services are designed to support clients in every aspect of the equestrian industry. Whether you are interested in purchasing or leasing horses outside of our facilities, we provide expert advisory services to guide you through the process. Additionally, we specialize in organizing and recommending appropriate transportation solutions, including trailers for horses and tack, ensuring seamless logistics and peace of mind. Trust our seasoned professionals to deliver unparalleled expertise and support for all your equestrian business needs.
