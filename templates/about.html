About

Welcome to Mad Maple Farm, our premier horse training and sales business. We specialize in hunter jumper English riding disciplines along the East Coast. Our mission is to provide exceptional horse care, training, competitive showing services, and ensuring both horse and rider reach their full potential.


With decades of experience in the horse industry and show circuits, we have worked with and competed along side some of the most prestigious riders and trainers in the world. allowing us to develop a deep understanding and expertise in equestrian sports. Our business is the result of merging several successful horse enterprises, creating an ultimate platform that caters to the diverse needs of our students and clients.


Located in the heart of Ocala, FL, just minutes away from the renowned World Equestrian Center and a short drive from Wellington, FL, our facility is ideally situated for equestrian enthusiasts. We specialize in traveling up and down the East Coast, participating in large shows and competitions, showcasing our expertise and dedication to the sport. Our students and clients come from diverse backgrounds and locations, and we are committed to providing them with an exceptional experience. Whether you are an advanced rider or a newcomer to the sport of hunter jumping, we tailor our services to meet your individual needs and goals.


Convenient Location:

    Distance to Orlando, FL: Approximately 80 miles, about a 1 hour and 20-minute drive.
    Driving Directions from Orlando to Ocala: Take I-75 S and FL-91 S for the fastest route. Alternatively, you can take US-441 S and SE Highway 42 for a scenic drive.

Join our community of passionate equestrians and experience the unparalleled expertise and support we offer. Our family-owned business, with deep roots in the horse industry, is dedicated to helping you achieve your equestrian dreams.
