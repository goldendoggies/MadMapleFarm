Farm and Facilities

Welcome to Mad Maple Farm

Mad Maple Farm is just minutes away from the renowned World Equestrian Center and HITS Ocala. We are an easy drive from Jacksonville, Tampa and Wellington, FL. Our facility is ideally situated for local and out of town customers. Our students and clients come from diverse backgrounds and locations, and we are committed to providing them with an exceptional experience. Whether you are an advanced rider or a newcomer to the sport of hunter jumping, we tailor our services to meet your individual needs and goals.

Wee Burn Farm- Daryl Zeigler Henning- Owner/Founder Located at Wee Burn Farm in Reddick FL, Mad Maple Farm is fortunate to have access to the bucolic setting and amenities offered by the farm.

Newly constructed, the Wee Burn Farm was developed by life long horsewoman Daryl Henning Zeigler to capture the natural light, breeze and aesthetic of the rolling hills of Reddick, FL. The Farm is a wonderful base for our horses, providing a relaxing environment to decompress from showing and also a terrific place to train.

Training Arena
Six 1/2 Acre paddocks

The Facility features-

  • - A 175ft x 300ft all weather footing training arena with Martin Collins fiber.
  • - Sixteen 12ft x 12ft stalls
  • - Six 1/2 acre paddocks with individual run in sheds and water
  • - Four smaller paddocks with individual water
  • - Three larger paddocks for our younger and retired horses with individual run-ins that can be closed as stalls; each with its own water
  • - Heated and cooled lounge with full bathrooms, kitchen, wifi and cable. Perfect for those who work remotely.
  • - Individual tack lockers for each owner/ rider.
  • - 30 acres for hacking
  • - Jump Field
  • - Big A$$ fans above the stalls to provide air flow and cooling during the summer months.
  • - Our facility is monitored by CCTV and completely enclosed by no-climb fencing and coded gate for security and safety.
