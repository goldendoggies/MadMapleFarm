Photos and Videos

Showcase of Excellence: Photos and Videos from Horse Shows Explore our gallery of stunning photos and captivating videos taken at horse shows up and down the East Coast. Our collection highlights the beauty and grace of our horses, the excitement of the competition, and the pride of winning ribbons, awards, and accolades.


What You'll Find:

    Horses in Action: Witness the elegance and athleticism of our horses as they compete in various events, showcasing their skills and training.
    Competition Highlights: Experience the thrill of the show ring with action shots and videos capturing the intensity and precision of each performance.
    Ribbons and Awards: Celebrate our achievements with images of the ribbons, trophies, and accolades earned by our talented horses and riders.
    Behind the Scenes: Get a glimpse of the preparation and dedication that goes into each competition, from grooming and tacking to warm-ups and training sessions.

Join us in celebrating the success and hard work of our equestrian team. Our gallery is a testament to the passion and commitment that drives us to excel in the world of horse shows. Enjoy the journey through our visual stories and be inspired by the magic of the equestrian sport.
