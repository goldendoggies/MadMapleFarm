Mad Maple Farms

Welcome to Mad Maple Farm, our premier hunter jumping horse business, where decades of experience and a passion for excellence converge. Our journey in the horse industry has been shaped by working with some of the most prestigious trainers, allowing us to develop a deep understanding and expertise in equestrian sports.

As a family-owned business, our roots run deep in the horse industry. Our families have dedicated their lives to the care, training, and success of horses and riders. This rich heritage has been the foundation upon which we have built our premier show barn, offering top-tier services to both advanced riders and those who are developing their skills.

Our business is the result of merging several successful horse enterprises, creating an ultimate platform that caters to the diverse needs of our students and clients. We are proud to offer a comprehensive range of services, from personalized training programs to horse sales and leasing options, all designed to help you achieve your equestrian goals.

Join our community and experience the unparalleled expertise and dedication that define our family business. Whether you are an experienced competitor or just beginning your journey in the hunter jumper world, we are here to support and guide you every step of the way.
