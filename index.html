MAD MAPLE FARM

Excellence in hunters, jumpers and training

Our program at Mad Maple Farm encompasses all aspects of training, management, and planning for both horse and rider. With decades of experience, our team excels in producing horses and riders to achieve their specific goals in a competitive, timely, and safe manner.

Our training and riding methods are honed through years of guidance from Olympic-level riders, trainers, veterinarians, and farriers. We have distilled this extensive experience into a unique and focused approach to competitive training and management.

We proudly offer a wide range of services, including personalized training programs, horse sales, and leasing options, all designed to help you achieve your equestrian goals. Our journey in the horse industry has been shaped by collaborations with some of the most prestigious riders and trainers, allowing us to develop deep expertise in equestrian sports.
