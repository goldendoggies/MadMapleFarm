Training Program

Our training program offers customized lessons and training for riders and horses of all experience levels. Personalized training programs are tailored for the specific needs of the individual horse and rider to accommodate their needs.

Our approach is founded in classical flat work and a systematic approach to jumping. Utilizing exercises that consistently present material to insure a seamless and confident journey.

Horse Management

At Mad Maple Farm we are committed to providing top-notch boarding services that cater to the needs of both horses and their owners. Success is in the details. The care of our equine partners is comprehensive and detailed, while still allowing them to be “horses”.

To insure that each horse is receiving the detailed care that we believe is necessary, Mad Maple has an experienced staff that learn and care for your horse.
